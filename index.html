“What if I’m off-schedule?”

Honest answers, before or after it happens. The only patch mistake that raises pregnancy risk is going more than 7 days with no hormones — so a late weekly change under 48h, or leaving a patch on too long, usually keeps you protected.

Patch schedule

The combined patch runs in a 28-day cycle: a new patch each week for 3 weeks, then a patch-free week (withdrawal bleed). Set the date you applied the first patch of your current cycle.

Security & data

Your data is stored only on this device, encrypted with your passcode (AES-256). Nothing is ever sent to any server.

What this protects: someone who gets your phone or a backup file can't read your data without the passcode. What it can't protect against: anyone who knows or can compel your passcode, or sees the app already unlocked. Use a passcode different from your phone PIN, and lock the app when you're done.

To keep a year+ of data safe from being wiped: tap “Export encrypted backup”, then in the Share/Save sheet choose Save to Files → iCloud Drive. The file is already encrypted, so even iCloud can't read it. Restore anytime with “Import backup”.

Petal is for personal tracking only and is not medical advice or a contraceptive guarantee. Predictions are estimates. Follow your patch's instructions and talk to a clinician with any concerns.
